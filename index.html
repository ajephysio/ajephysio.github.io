About Ivan

Physiotherapy as a genuine partnership

I'm Ivan, an AHPRA-registered physiotherapist based in western Brisbane. Originally from Singapore, I bring a warm, person-centred approach to every visit — because recovery works best when we work on it together.

I specialise in mobile and community-based care for NDIS participants, older adults, and people who prefer treatment in the comfort of their own home.
