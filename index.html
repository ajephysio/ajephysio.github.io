About Ivan

Physiotherapy as a genuine partnership

I'm Ivan, a physiotherapist based in western Brisbane, and I care deeply about helping people feel supported, understood, and confident in their recovery. I believe physiotherapy works best when there is trust, kindness, and a genuine partnership between therapist and client.

I provide mobile physiotherapy for NDIS participants, older adults, and anyone who feels more comfortable receiving care at home. My goal is to make each session practical, encouraging, and centred around what matters most to you, so that progress feels achievable and meaningful.
